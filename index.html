ABOUT US

A Commitment to
Legal Excellence

BNK Legal Consultancy Ltd is a London-based legal consultancy firm, incorporated in 2021, dedicated to delivering precise, practical, and commercially-minded legal advice.

We partner with our clients — from startups to established businesses — to navigate complex legal challenges with confidence. Our approach combines rigorous legal expertise with a deep understanding of business realities, ensuring our advice is not only legally sound but commercially relevant.

Registered and operating in England and Wales, we serve clients across the United Kingdom and internationally, offering a full spectrum of legal consultancy services tailored to each client's unique circumstances.

OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Legal
Consultancy Services

We provide a wide range of legal consultancy services, tailored to protect and advance your interests in an ever-changing legal landscape.

Corporate Advisory

Strategic legal counsel for businesses at every stage — from incorporation and corporate structuring to governance, compliance, and mergers & acquisitions.

Contract Management

Expert drafting, reviewing, and negotiating of commercial contracts, partnership agreements, supplier agreements, and service contracts.

Regulatory Compliance

Helping businesses navigate complex regulatory frameworks including GDPR, anti-money laundering (AML), and sector-specific compliance requirements.

Employment Law

Comprehensive employment law advice for both employers and employees — covering contracts, HR policies, redundancy, and workplace disputes.

Immigration Advisory

Business immigration support including sponsor licences, skilled worker visas, global talent routes, and international mobility strategies.

Dispute Resolution

Expert guidance through mediation, arbitration, and litigation processes — helping clients resolve disputes efficiently and cost-effectively.
