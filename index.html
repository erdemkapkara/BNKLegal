Our Services

Turkish Legal & Business Advisory

Turkish legal consultancy, cross-border business support, commercial documentation and strategic guidance tailored to your needs.

The services below provide a general overview and are not exhaustive. Each matter is considered individually. To find out whether your matter falls within our scope, please contact us with a brief summary of the assistance required.

01

Turkish Legal Advisory

Legal advice, research and practical support in relation to Turkish legal, corporate and commercial matters, including coordination with and referral to suitably qualified Turkish lawyers where specialist advice or representation is required.

02

Business Establishment & Strategic Support

Support for individuals and businesses seeking to establish, acquire, develop or expand a business in Türkiye, as well as general business, administrative and documentary support for entrepreneurs developing commercial activities involving the United Kingdom.

03

Corporate & Commercial Advisory

Assistance with corporate structures, shareholder arrangements, partnerships, corporate governance and commercial relationships involving Türkiye, including cross-border business arrangements between Türkiye and the United Kingdom.

04

Contract Drafting & Commercial Documentation

Preparation, review and revision of contracts, agreements and commercial documents relating to Turkish law matters and cross-border business arrangements, including identification of key legal and commercial risks and coordination with relevant professional advisers where required.

05

Cross-Border Business & Market Entry Support

Strategic and practical support for businesses trading, investing, collaborating or entering into commercial relationships between the United Kingdom and Türkiye, including assistance with market entry, documentation, negotiations and coordination between the relevant parties and professional advisers.

06

Legal, Corporate & Bilingual Document Support

Preparation and review of legal, corporate and commercial documents, including legal research, written guidance, formal correspondence, bilingual drafting and terminology review. We also assist with notarisation, apostille and legalisation requirements and coordinate with appropriately qualified translators where certified or sworn translation is required.

About Us

BNK Legal Consultancy Ltd

BNK Legal Consultancy Ltd is an independent legal and business consultancy founded by Busra Nur Kapkara and Erdem Kapkara and incorporated in England and Wales in March 2021.

We provide Turkish legal consultancy and cross-border business support to individuals, entrepreneurs and companies based in the United Kingdom, Türkiye and internationally. Our work is particularly focused on clients who have legal or commercial interests in Türkiye, including those establishing or developing a business, working with Turkish companies, entering into partnerships, reviewing commercial arrangements or requiring assistance with Turkish legal and corporate matters.

Busra Nur Kapkara

Director & Co-Founder

Busra Nur Kapkara is an Avukat admitted to the Istanbul Bar Association and a practising Solicitor of England and Wales. She leads the company's legal and consultancy work and is responsible for its professional direction and client matters.

Erdem Kapkara

Director & Co-Founder

Erdem Kapkara oversees the company's day-to-day administrative and operational support, including the coordination of enquiries, appointments, documentation, records and invoicing. He also liaises with clients and external professionals on administrative matters. He does not provide legal advice.

Since 2021, we have supported clients with a range of legal, contractual, corporate and business-related matters. Much of our work comes through personal recommendations, returning clients and established professional relationships.

We work directly with our clients and do not offer standardised online packages. Each enquiry is reviewed individually before instructions are accepted. Depending on the client's location and the nature of the matter, consultations may take place in person, by email, by telephone or by video conference.

Please be aware that BNK Legal Consultancy Ltd is not authorised or regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority as a law firm. Busra Nur Kapkara is a practising Solicitor of England and Wales and is individually authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority. Her individual authorisation is separate from the regulatory status of BNK Legal Consultancy Ltd and does not mean that the company itself is an SRA-authorised practice.
